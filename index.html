About Me

Welcome to my portfolio! My name is Hashir Owais, and I am a third-year student at the University of Regina, pursuing a degree in Software Systems Engineering with a minor in Electronics Systems Engineering. My academic journey is focused on a concentration in Microelectronics, reflecting my deep interest in the intersection of hardware and software.

Academic Interests

Throughout my studies, I have developed a broad set of skills and interests, ranging from software development to microelectronics. I am passionate about exploring various fields, including:

  • Software Development: Crafting efficient and innovative software solutions.
  • Microelectronics (Embedded Systems): Diving deep into the design and development of embedded systems.
  • IT/Network Security: Ensuring the safety and integrity of data and systems.
  • Networking/Network Engineering/Telecommunications: Building and maintaining robust communication networks.
  • QA Quality Testing: Ensuring the highest standards of quality in software and hardware products.

Beyond the Classroom

Outside of my academic pursuits, I enjoy spending time with friends, going on hikes, and working on cars. I am also starting to get into 3D printing and creating gadgets with microcontrollers. These activities not only provide a balance to my rigorous academic schedule but also allow me to explore my interests and passions in a relaxed and enjoyable manner.
